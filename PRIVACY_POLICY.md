## Overview

MetalCalc Pro is a professional metal weight calculator and cost estimation tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our app handles your information.

## Data Collection

**MetalCalc Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal user data.**

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Financial or payment information
- User behavior data
- Crash reports or diagnostics data

## Data Storage

All calculations and data entered into MetalCalc Pro are processed entirely on your device. The app does not send any data to external servers, cloud services, or third parties. Any values you enter (dimensions, weights, prices) exist only within the app session and are not persisted or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

MetalCalc Pro does not integrate with or use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data, including but not limited to:

- Advertising networks
- Analytics platforms
- Social media sharing
- Cloud storage services
- Payment processors

## Permissions

MetalCalc Pro does not request any device permissions. The app does not access:

- Camera or microphone
- Photo library
- Location services
- Contacts
- Calendar
- Health data
- Bluetooth
- NFC

## Children's Privacy

MetalCalc Pro is suitable for all ages and does not collect any data from children or any other users. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

## Security

Since MetalCalc Pro does not collect or transmit any data, there is minimal security risk. All computations are performed locally on your device.
