# Privacy Policy for MetalCalc Pro

**Last updated: May 28, 2026**

## Introduction

MetalCalc Pro ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**MetalCalc Pro does not collect any personal information.**

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or any other type of user information.

## How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, there is no data usage to describe. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

## Data Storage

All data entered into MetalCalc Pro (such as dimensions, material selections, and cost estimates) is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any external server or third party.

## Third-Party Services

MetalCalc Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The App operates entirely offline.

## Children's Privacy

MetalCalc Pro is suitable for all ages. Since we collect no personal information, our App is safe for users of any age.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
